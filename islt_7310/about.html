Hi! My name is Julian Fletcher. This is my third year at Mizzou, and I’m studying computer science! Additionally, I’m taking classes in geography, math, and IT for my minors. I’m very involved on campus. I’m the Vice President of RHA, a peer mentor in the College of Engineering, and a Resident Advisor. I love everything I’m involved in. They allow me to meet new people and make campus a better place! Missouri is currently my home, but I’m originally from Minnesota! I chose Mizzou because I wanted to go to school out of state, and this was the closest school I liked! I plan to get my master’s degree and PhD in computer science. I plan to focus my research on programming languages or graph algorithms. Once I’m done with school, I hope to work in a research lab in the industry or become a professor and teach someday!
